The World's Greatest Lasagna (Maybe)

Description

Sink your pearly whites into this decedant lasagna. Feel your tongue burn to a crispy goodness after the 600 degree tomato sauce your 97 degree tongue meet in the middle at 300 degrees fahrenheit... ouch

What a fantastic recipe for those chilly fall evenings, super bowl parties, or even a cookout!

Home Page

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Italian sausage, or ground beef
  • Mozzarella, Ricotta, Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Basil
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Prepare meat sauce
  4. Combine cheese mixture
  5. Layer & bake for 45 minutes
