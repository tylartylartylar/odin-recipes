Ingredients

  • Frozen Blueberries
  • Almond Milk, or 2% milk
  • Vanilla Protein Powder
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of frozen blueberries into your blender
  2. place 1 whole banana into the blender
  3. place 1 scoop of protein powder into the blender
  4. pour almond milk or milk into the blender
  5. Shake the blender to ensure the protein powder doesn't stick to the bottom of the cup
  6. Blend until smooth color and consistency and enjoy
